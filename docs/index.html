DiscoverSUTD is a 3 week long introduction to the SUTD community. It will last from 22nd May - 7 June 2019. This event is the culmination of 4 months of continuous coordination amongst 5th rows, faculty, pillars, housing, and research labs.

The main event page serves as the starting point for your SUTD journey. Within this page, there is a community calendar, session sign ups and speaker biographies for our flagship event, the SUTD Human Library.

DiscoverSUTD adopts the model of a Conference with large-scale talks targeted at the general audience but also breakout sessions that participants can sign-up for on an individualised manner. This means you need to sign up for events!

Tl;dr

  • All aspects of SUTD is represented in these first 3 weeks
  • It is a starting point for your SUTD journey
  • You need to sign up for individual events!
